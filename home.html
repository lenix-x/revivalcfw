About Us

What Makes Us Different

  • Custom Scripts & Features: We’ve implemented exclusive scripts to enhance your experience, from unique jobs and custom vehicles to personalized player interactions.
  • Roleplay Focus (if applicable): Immerse yourself in an authentic roleplaying environment where your choices matter. Whether you're a law-abiding citizen, a daring criminal, or something in between, your story unfolds as you shape the world
  • Active Economy: Take part in an evolving economy with various jobs, businesses, and criminal activities. Work your way up from nothing to owning luxury cars, businesses, and properties.
  • Dynamic Events: Our server offers regular events, including races, roleplaying scenarios, community meetups, and server-wide heists. There's always something happening!
  • Friendly & Active Community: Join a welcoming community with active admins and moderators ensuring fair play and helping newcomers. Whether you’re a seasoned roleplayer or new to FiveM, we have room for you.

Key Features:

  • Custom Vehicles & Maps: Explore a world with handpicked custom vehicles and unique locations not found in standard GTA V.
  • Law Enforcement & Emergency Services: Join the police, medical services, or even become a firefighter. Our server offers a realistic approach to public service roles.
  • Criminal Enterprises: For those seeking excitement, venture into the world of crime with gang operations, heists, and drug trades. Just be careful not to get caught!
  • Player-owned Businesses: Start your own business, from a car dealership to a nightclub, and watch as the community thrives around you.
  • Realistic Economy: Work hard for your money or find more “creative” ways to earn it.
